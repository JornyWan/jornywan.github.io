About me

Hi there! I am Cheng Wan and currently a first year Master student of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at Georgia Tech.

Before starting my Master’s degree, I did research in the biomedical area, specifically in the field of blood pressure measurement at the Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology of the Chinese Academy of Science (SIAT/CAS).

I am interested in the application of deep learning, particularly in the areas of speech and natural language processing (NLP). I also have an interest in machine learning and the biomedical field.

In terms of some professional achievements, I have submitted a paper to the Journal of Integration Technology and am currently preparing for the incoming IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Conference (EMBC) 2023.

I am excited to continue exploring my interests and furthering my knowledge and expertise in these fields. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me!
